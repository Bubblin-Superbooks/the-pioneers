They do say, too, there is a power of goold and silver in the place—the Lord forgive me for setting my heart on woorldly things; but what falls in the battle, there's scriptur' for believing, is the just property of the victor.”

“Retreating!” exclaimed the amazed veteran; “where's my horse? he has been shot under me—I——”
